Kitaro - Kojiki & The Universe 2017

Kitaro is a Japanese recording artist, composer, record producer best known for his electronic-instrumental music. He is often regarded as one of the most prominent names in the new-age music scene. In 1986, he signed with Geffen Records for a worldwide distribution arrangement.

By 1987, he had collaborated with various notable musicians like Micky Hart and Jon Andersen. Having a strong fanbase with 10 million records sold worldwide, Kitaro kicked off his world tour from 2007 to 2011. Passionate about giving back, Kitaro donated a portion of the proceeds from his album sales to charity.
